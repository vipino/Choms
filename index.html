CHOMS Coconut oil is a pure coconut oil made from Kerala, which is famous for its coconut industry. The product is made by Chettoor Oil Mills using hygienically produced copra bought straight from farmers. ‘Choms’ is a quality driven company engaged in the production of a superb range of coconut oil that is not just genuine but also healthy. Chettoor’s pure choms coconut oils are hygenically extracted from selected, best quality edible Copra(dry coconut).We are always conscious about the standard of the products. Besides the strict control and supervision of every stage, right from the procurement of row copra to end product,we assure the high quality.

CHOMS Coconut oil is made from copra directly procured from farmers . The coconut oil is extracted in hygienic conditions with no added preservatives or chemicals. The coconut oil undergoes a three step purification process before it reaches the hands of the consumers. Every effort is made to preserve the natural aroma of coconuts.
